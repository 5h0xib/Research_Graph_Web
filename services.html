Our Services

Comprehensive research solutions tailored to your business needs

Research Graph offers a full suite of market research and data analytics services designed to provide actionable insights for strategic decision-making. Our experienced team combines traditional research methodologies with cutting-edge technology to deliver superior results.

Survey Programming

Precision scripting for complex research needs

We handle simple to highly complex surveys across all major platforms including ConfirmIT, Decipher, and Qualtrics. Our scripting ensures logic precision, multi-language overlay, and seamless mobile compatibility for a superior respondent experience.

Key Capabilities:
  • ConfirmIT, Decipher, Qualtrics expertise
  • Complex logic & conjoint analysis
  • Multi-language support
  • Mobile-first design

Data Quality & Analytics

Rigorous validation for reliable insights

Beyond standard cleaning, we implement rigorous quality checks including digital fingerprinting, open-end analysis, and speeder checks. Our analytics team delivers advanced statistical outputs, cross-tabs, and predictive modelling.

Services Include:
  • Digital fingerprinting & fraud detection
  • Advanced data cleaning
  • Multivariate analysis
  • Predictive modelling

Insight Narratives & Strategic Recommendations

Moving beyond data to drive strategy

We don't just deliver tables; we tell the story behind the data. Our reports provide clear, thematic analysis and actionable strategic recommendations that help stakeholders make confident decisions.

Deliverables:
  • Executive summaries
  • Thematic analysis
  • Visual storytelling
  • Strategic workshops

High-Quality Respondent Access & Field Control

Access to verified audiences worldwide

We provide access to deeply engaged B2B and B2C panels globally. Our field control includes real-time monitoring and strict validation protocols to ensure only genuine respondents enter your study.

Field Features:
  • Verified B2B & B2C panels
  • Global reach
  • Real-time monitoring
  • Strict validation protocols

Seamless Delivery & Cross-Market Coordination

Unified project management for global studies

For multi-country trackers and ad-hoc studies, we offer a single point of contact and 24/7 project management. We harmonize data across markets to ensure consistency and comparability.

Management Benefits:
  • Single Point of Contact (SPOC)
  • 24/7 "Follow the Sun" operations
  • Multi-country harmonization
  • Agile delivery

Data Dashboards & Visualisation
